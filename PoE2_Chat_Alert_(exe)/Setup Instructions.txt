1) Click "Select Log File": You need to use the Client.txt file in the Path of Exile 2 logs folder.

     For Steam: Go to where Steam is installed on your PC.
           Steam --> steamapps --> common --> Path of Exile 2 --> logs --> client.txt

     For GGG Launcher go to where Path of Exile 2 was installed then follow the steps above.

2) Click "Select Sound File"
	- Supports .mp3 or .wav files.

3) Enable.





Note: You can hit the "x" on the top right and it will minimize to tray.
      You do not need to keep the application window open.
